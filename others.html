My Guitars Collection
2021-
Martin OM-28 John Mayer

This guitar was luckily found and purchased through a friend (also a big fan of John Mayer) working in a guitar shop in Taipei, Taiwan. The guitar is the first official John Mayer Signature model made by Martin and was produced as a limited run of only 404, and I own the #173. The guitar features a solid Engelmann spruce top and solid East Indian rosewood back and sides.

2012-
Martin HD-28

This guitar was purchased for celebrating my 20-year-old birthday and is the one that introduces me to Martin Guitar. After years of practicing, at that moment, I thought it is time to have an all-solid guitar, and luckily I found this pre-owned Martin at a guitar shop in Tainan, Taiwan. The guitar features a solid Sitka spruce top and solid East Indian rosewood back and sides.

2011-
YAMAHA F310

This is my first acoustic guitar. I purchased it at an inconspicuous local guitar shop near my home in Tainan, Taiwan.

2016-
Fender Japan Stratocaster, 1993

This pre-owned guitar is a gift from my aunt. The guitar features a Floyd Rose vibrato system. While this guitar is super unplayable due to its broken vibrato system, which makes it easily out of tune.

2016-2021
Martin D-18 Gorden Era

This guitar was purchased during my graduation trip to Fukuoka, Japan in 2016. The guitar features a solid Adirondack spruce top and Genuine Mahogany back and sides. The guitar was later sold due to its playability, vintage V-shape neck making me hard to play JM's song. The sound of this guitar is pretty amzing though.

2018-2021
Fender American Vintage '52 Telecaster

This guitar was purchased at a famous guitar shop in Taipei, Taiwan. It is my first electric guitar, officially. At that moment, I felt like playing JM's electric licks. Despite I had sold it since I later realized I am just an acoustic guy lol.
