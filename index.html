Graduate Research Assistant, OSU ECE

Hello, my name is Kevin Michael Chen, as known as "Acicula" Chen, or Chen Chi-Wen (hanzi:陳啟文). I am currently a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at The Ohio State University, advised by Prof. Parinaz Naghizadeh and co-advised by Prof. Aylin Yener. Prior to my PhD study, I had three years of research experience in machine learning and wireless network communication as a fully-time research assistant advised by Dr. Ronlad Y. Chang at Research Center for Information Technology Innovation (CITI), Academia Sinica, Taiwan.

Apart from the academic activity, I am an amateur guitar player, having 10 more years of self-practicing guitar. My favorite guitarist, who inspires and influences me the most, is John Mayer. I am proud of having his signature Martin guitar, the Martin OM28 JM. Check out here for my JM covers.
