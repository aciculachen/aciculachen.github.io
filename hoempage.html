About me

A PhD student at OSU

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at The Ohio State Universitry, advised by Prof. Parinaz Naghizadeh and co-advised by Prof. Aylin Yener.


Prior to my PhD study, I had three years of research experience in machine learning and wireless network communication as a fully-time research assistent advised by Prof. Ronlad Y. Chang at Research Center for Information Technology Innovation (CITI), Academia Sinica, Taiwan.


I received my MS degree from National Taiwan University (NTU, 國立臺灣大學), Taiwan. I received my BS degree from National Cheng Kung University (NCKU, 國立成功大學), Taiwan.
